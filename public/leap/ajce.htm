2006 - 2024 Journey
  • 2006 - ED Cell started under T-TBI Govt. of Kerala
  • 2009 - First IEDC in Kerala by DST, Govt. of India
  • 2013 - IPR Cell Sanctioned by KSCSTE, Govt. of Kerala
  • 2015 - IEDC Bootcamp by KSUM, Govt. of Kerala
  • 2015 - NSTEDB TBI by DST, Govt. of India
  • 2018 - Institution Innovation Council with the support of AICTE, Govt of India
  • 2020 - BIRAC BioNEST by DBT, Govt. of India
  • 2024 - LEAP by KSUM
  • 2024 - Campus Industrial Park

Objectives

An overriding objective of the venture is to achieve a transformation of minds of the students from being job-seekers to being entrepreneurs themselves, utilizing the theoretical and practical lessons they get at the College.

  • Creation of Technology-based incubates on a continuous basis.
  • Help to create value-added jobs and services.
  • Introduction of an entrepreneurial culture to students and the general public.
  • Create effective networking for the development of technology-based start-ups.
  • Develop internationally accepted technologies.
  • Promote students to come up with commercially viable curriculum projects.
  • Create student entrepreneurs.
  • Create awareness about Technology Incubation and Commercialization of R & D products and processes.
  • Promote small and medium industries.

Thrust Areas

Startup Valley, the first TBI, concentrates on the following thrust areas.

  • Rural technology
  • Food Technology
  • Green Technology
  • Information Technology
  • Artificial Intelligence

About us

We initiated the entrepreneurial journey by starting an IEDC under the mentorship of TTBI, Trivandrum. As a result of this great beginning, many of our students came up with innovative ventures. To realize the inspiration and enthusiasm of our student community into marketable products, it was essential to scale up the IEDC to the level of a TBI.

  • First BIRAC BioNEST TBI in Kerala, funded by the Department of Bio-Technology, Govt. of India.
  • ARTBI - Approved and supported by the Department of Science and Technology, Govt. of India
  • AJCE is the first self-financing college under the KTU to get this multi-crore (₹3.65 crore) project from Govt of India.
  • One of the new generation engineering colleges in Kerala to get this prestigious center from the Govt. of India.
  • Thought-provoking atmosphere with world-class infrastructure, mentoring support, Library, Laboratory, testing facility, and dedicated 610 Mbps internet facility.

Our Aim

  • An overriding objective of the venture is to achieve a transformation of minds of the students from being job-seekers to being entrepreneurs themselves, utilizing the theoretical and practical lessons they get at the College.
  • The TBI marks the beginning of a stronger industry-academia-consumer linkage where ideas can flow to and fro through the three stakeholder groups so that everyone benefits. Industry will benefit from technological developments initiated by the TBI as well as qualified students who have an innovative mind to join their workforce. Academia will gain from its constant interaction with industry, and consumers stand to gain from the innovations that are rolled out through the association.
